# Deploy to Railway - Quick Guide

Your app is ready to deploy! Follow these simple steps:

## Step 1: Go to Railway
1. Open: https://railway.app/new
2. Sign up/login (free account)

## Step 2: Deploy
1. Click **"Empty Project"**
2. Upload your project files (or connect GitHub)
3. Railway will auto-detect Node.js
4. Click **Deploy**

## Step 3: Environment Variables (Optional)
Your app will work without these, but add if needed:
- `DATABASE_URL` - (Railway can auto-provide PostgreSQL)
- `OPENAI_API_KEY` - For Michelle AI features
- `PORT` - Railway sets this automatically

## Step 4: Get Your URL
1. Go to your Railway project
2. Click **"Generate Domain"** 
3. Your app will be live!

Executive Summary (non-technical)

Most employers struggle to manage health risks before hiring. Small issues missed at pre-employment often lead to costly WorkCover claims later.

GPNet automates this step:
	1.	Candidates complete a health form.
	2.	The system analyses their responses.
	3.	Employers receive a clear report with Fit Classification and recommendations.

Everything is stored in Freshdesk tickets, ensuring a full audit trail.
This document specifies how the process works, how data flows, and how success will be measured.
